# 📊 Customer Segmentation Dashboard Using RFM Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

Customer Segmentation is a data analytics project that groups customers based on their purchasing behavior using the RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) model.

This interactive dashboard helps businesses identify valuable customers, understand customer behavior, improve retention strategies, and optimize marketing campaigns.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

* Analyze customer purchasing behavior
* Calculate RFM metrics
* Segment customers into meaningful groups
* Identify high-value customers
* Detect customers at risk of churn
* Generate business insights and recommendations
* Create an interactive web dashboard

---

## 📖 What is RFM Analysis?

### Recency (R)

Measures how recently a customer made a purchase.

### Frequency (F)

Measures how often a customer purchases.

### Monetary (M)

Measures how much money a customer spends.

RFM analysis helps businesses understand customer value and create targeted marketing strategies.

---

## 👥 Customer Segments

### 🏆 Champions

Customers who purchase frequently, recently, and spend the most.

### 💎 Loyal Customers

Regular customers who consistently purchase products.

### 🌟 Potential Loyalists

Customers who show strong engagement and may become loyal customers.

### ⚠️ At-Risk Customers

Customers whose purchasing activity is declining.

### ❌ Lost Customers

Customers who have stopped purchasing for a long period.
