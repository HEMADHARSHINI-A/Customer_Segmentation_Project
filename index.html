Project Overview

This project analyzes customer purchasing behavior using Recency, Frequency and Monetary (RFM) Analysis. Customers are grouped into different segments to help businesses improve retention, increase revenue and optimize marketing strategies.

Objectives

  • Analyze customer purchasing patterns
  • Calculate RFM scores
  • Segment customers into groups
  • Identify high-value customers
  • Provide business recommendations
