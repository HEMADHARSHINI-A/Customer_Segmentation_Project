Business Insights & Recommendations

Customer Segment Business Insight Recommendation Expected Impact Priority
🏆 Champions Generate the highest revenue and purchase frequently. Provide VIP rewards, exclusive offers and referral programs. Increase customer lifetime value. High
💎 Loyal Customers Consistently purchase products and engage with the brand. Offer premium memberships and personalized recommendations. Improve retention and repeat purchases. High
🚀 Potential Loyalists Show strong purchasing behavior but are not yet loyal. Send targeted promotions and loyalty program invitations. Convert them into loyal customers. Medium
⚠️ At Risk Customer engagement and purchase frequency are declining. Launch win-back campaigns and special discounts. Reduce customer churn. High
❌ Lost Customers No recent transactions and very low engagement. Run reactivation emails and limited-time offers. Recover inactive customers. Medium
🆕 New Customers Recently acquired customers with limited purchase history. Provide onboarding offers and welcome campaigns. Increase early retention. Medium
