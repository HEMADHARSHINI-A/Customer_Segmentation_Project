🔍 Key Findings

  • Champions generate the highest revenue contribution.
  • Loyal customers provide stable recurring revenue.
  • Potential Loyalists can be converted into Champions through targeted campaigns.
  • At-Risk customers show decreasing purchase frequency.
  • Lost customers represent a churn risk for the business.

💡 Business Recommendations

  • Offer exclusive rewards to Champions.
  • Provide loyalty points to Loyal Customers.
  • Create personalized offers for Potential Loyalists.
  • Run email campaigns for At-Risk Customers.
  • Provide discount coupons to reactivate Lost Customers.

📈 Conclusion

RFM Analysis successfully identified customer segments based on purchasing behavior. The analysis helps businesses improve customer retention, increase revenue, optimize marketing strategies, and focus on high-value customers.
